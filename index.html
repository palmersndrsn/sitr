Singin' in the Rain

The Power of One

The purpose for creating and continuing SINGIN’ IN THE RAIN-RADIO is to keep the torch of Hope and the Good News burning brightly. By sharing the powerful activities and love of spectacular guests, our intention is to up level conscious awareness, inspire expansive behavior and create more sacred action steps in our global viewers.

‘Sages’ of the show who range from the internationally known such as Marianne Williamson, Panache Desai and Oprah’s many Ambassadors of Hope to the heroes of small African townships such as Frederick Ndabaramiye and Dr. Samuel Sasu, breathe life into bold new possibilities. True Social Activism!

The “Make a Difference” friends of SINGIN’ IN THE RAIN-RADIO range from artists, authors, philosophers and scientists to leaders in high impact NGOs who come to share their brilliant, generous and sincere desire to shape our future. The bold dreams, the information and the myth busting are brought through humor, story and song. Knowing that there’s always more, we continuously ask the question, what is our part to play in this next step moving to a beautiful, peaceful and sustainable existence? “What’s Next to Do?”
